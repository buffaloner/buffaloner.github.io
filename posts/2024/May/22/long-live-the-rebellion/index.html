Long Live the Rebellion

Posted on Wed 22 May 2024 in News

There was a news story I came across recently about schools banning cell phones. I remember growing up when they implemented metal detectors. Kids can be cruel, and hormones reek havoc on your mind as well as your complexion. It's hard enough to pay attention to the lessons being taught when your mind wanders due to uncontrollable urges. But when you do get a moment of clarity, if you're not distracted by buzzing or waiting on the hottest gossip, you might just be able to pay attention to something longer than a ~~Vine~~ TikTok reel.

Now if only we could get drivers to be as studious when bikers are sharing the road.
