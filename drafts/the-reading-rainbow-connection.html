The Reading Rainbow Connection

Posted on Sat 27 July 2024 in News

Did you know that Reading Rainbow was created in Buffalo, New York? I would like to say I had a little part in it. Very little. Like microscopic at the beginning, but a little bigger towards its airing. I mean to say, while I never met Tom Buttino, I know we were in the same room at some point. My mother worked at WNED in the early 80s with Tom while he worked to produce the series. She was pregnant with me while she worked there in 1981. I doubt I was much help in getting it off the ground.

She instilled in me a love for learning and an obsession with books. I still remember finding the ones she read in college up in the attic. I still have them on my bookshelf. She also engendered a passion for artistic expression in me. She passed in 2007, but in the years after her cancer prognosis, she dived back into the arts that she had set aside while raising children. I have many of her batiks hanging on my walls, as well as watercolor and oil paintings she made in the little art studio she setup by the laundry room in the basement.

Her work is what inspired me to create BuffaLoner Brand and spread the message of kindness she showed everyone in her life and her work.
